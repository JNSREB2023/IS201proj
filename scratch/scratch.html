How to make Butternut Squash Mac & Cheese (Like the Trader-Joes one)


Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 1 package of your favorite pasta
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1-2 cups of cheese (depends on how much you like cheese)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Cut the butternut squash in half and scoop out the seeds.
  3. Place the squash (cut side down), onion, and garlic on a baking sheet and roast for 30-40 minutes, or until tender.
  4. While the squash is roasting, cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  5. Once the squash is done, scoop out the flesh and add it to a pot with the onion and garlic.
  6. Add the vegetable broth and use an emersion blender to blend everything.
    • Note: If you don't have an emersion blender, you can use a regular blender, but be careful not to burn yourself
  7. Add the heavy cream, cheese and cooked pasta, stirring to combine.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
